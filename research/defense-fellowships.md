## Clearance as a Differentiator — Where It Actually Matters

Active Secret clearance eliminates most of the applicant pool at:
- **Lockheed Martin Fort Worth** — listed as a requirement, not a preference
- **BAE Systems Manassas** — Northern Virginia cleared contractor environment
- **GDLS Sterling Heights** — classified armor programs
- **Boeing classified programs** — defense programs require clearance eligibility at minimum
- **DEVCOM ARL** — classified materials research (armor, energetics, ballistics)
- **NREIP/NRL** — Navy classified research programs

TS interim is an additional differentiator at:
- **Lockheed Space (Sunnyvale)** — classified satellite/space programs
- **AFRL** — many AFRL programs are TS/SCI-adjacent
- **DARPA** — all work is classified or near-classified
