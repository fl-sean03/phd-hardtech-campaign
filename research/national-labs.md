## Notes on Defense Clearance Advantage

Active Secret + TS Interim is a decisive differentiator at LANL, LLNL, Sandia, and ARL DEVCOM. These weapons labs frequently have candidates otherwise qualified who cannot be placed because clearance processing takes months. Candidates with active clearance eliminate that bottleneck entirely. In every cover letter for these labs, clearance status should be in the first paragraph.

For SCGSR: frame clearance as enabling access to a broader range of DOE lab research projects, including work at restricted facilities.
