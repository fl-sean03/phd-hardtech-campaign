### Clearance Positioning for All Applications
Candidates holding active Secret clearance (and especially TS interim) have a **hard differentiator** at every FFRDC. Lead every cover letter with a direct statement of clearance status and the practical implication: no processing ramp-up time, immediate access to export-controlled work, and a demonstrated history of operating in DoD-adjacent technical environments.

### Key Framing for FFRDC Applications
- Frame any prior DoD or national lab simulation work as: computational result → manufacturing output → operational relevance (the full translation chain)
- Simulation experience on DoD HPC clusters = direct credibility signal for any DoD-adjacent lab
- Publications in preparation with DoD co-authors are evidence of rigor
- Automated simulation workflow projects signal autonomous capability — valuable for FFRDCs trying to modernize their simulation workflows
