## Notes on Clearance as Differentiator

**Sectors where Secret/TS is a direct differentiator:**
- Energetics (Firehawk, any DoD propellant company) — often requires clearance for floor access
- Defense FFRDC (Aerospace Corporation, MITRE) — clearance = career acceleration
- SNC, Safran, and other defense primes at secure Colorado facilities
- Velo3D (DIU's additive manufacturing partner for munitions production)

**Sectors where clearance is nice-to-have but not a filter:**
- Commercial launch (Ursa Major, Venus Aerospace, Firefly, Rocket Lab)
- eVTOL (Joby Aviation)
- Construction tech (ICON)

**Clearance positioning**: Most students applying for engineering internships do not have an active Secret clearance. Clearance-required roles at SNC, Safran, and Aerospace Corporation have significantly thinner applicant pools. The TS interim is a bonus — it signals an ongoing investigation that most interns would take months to initiate. Lead with this when the posting mentions clearance eligibility.
